AI And Automation

The importance of automation and AI is further presented in regard to manual labor. Human work is often mundane and repetitive. It can also require years of training to perfect skill and often requires valuable resources. Humans are also prone to mistakes and in dire situations, may lead to death. Human error is often the key factor in inferior tasks and a waste of resources. Through ai and automation processes, the human factor is eradicated. This ensures work is completed in a timely fashion. Is accurate and quality is held to a high standard.
