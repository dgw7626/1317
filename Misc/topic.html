Automation in modern technology society

Automation technology is built to do a repetitive job from human work, it requires humans to manipulate the automation process. This technology has less-cost, 24 hours active machinery. Negative side of this technology is it needs specific order to proceed with some kind of action. Ports of Auckland is a sea ports operator company. They use automation to move containers in the right place but automation progress has difficulty moving to unrecognizable places. Their operators have to manually proceed to put unrecognizable places. Automation cannot respond to sudden changes to unpredictable variables from the environment.
